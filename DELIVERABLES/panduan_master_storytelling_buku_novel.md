## Metodologi Implementasi Praktis

### **Phase 1: Pre-Writing (Foundation)**
1. **Develop One-Page Premise**
   - Character: Who is the protagonist?
   - Situation: What world/setting?
   - Goal: What do they want?
   - Opposition: Who/what blocks them?
   - Stakes: Worst-case scenario if they fail?

2. **Audience & Genre Definition**
   - Target demographic
   - Genre expectations & promises
   - Competition analysis

3. **Voice Development**
   - Character monologue exercises
   - Different tone variations
   - Dialogue consistency testing

### **Phase 2: Planning (Blueprint)**
1. **Three-Act Structure Outline**
   - Act I (25%): Setup, inciting incident, plot point 1
   - Act II (50%): Confrontation, midpoint twist, plot point 2
   - Act III (25%): Climax, resolution, denouement

2. **Synopsis Development**
   - Short synopsis (1 paragraph): Overall story arc
   - Long synopsis (4 pages): Scene-by-scene breakdown
   - Character relationship mapping

3. **Character Development Worksheets**
   - Want (conscious goal) vs Need (unconscious growth)
   - Greatest fear & fatal flaw
   - Distinctive voice & speech patterns
   - Character arc transformation

### **Phase 3: Writing (Draft)**
1. **Daily Writing Routine**
   - Minimum word count (500-1000 words/day)
   - Consistency over perfection
   - "Shitty first draft" philosophy

2. **Scene Writing Structure**
   - Goal: What does character want in this scene?
   - Conflict: What opposes their goal?
   - Outcome: How does scene change situation?
   - Transition: How does it lead to next scene?

3. **Dialogue Best Practices**
   - Read aloud for natural flow
   - Subtext over exposition
   - Character voice consistency
   - Purpose: advance plot or reveal character

### **Phase 4: Revision (Polish)**
1. **Structural Revision**
   - Plot holes & timeline consistency
   - Character motivation clarity
   - Pacing & rhythm analysis
   - Theme integration check

2. **Line Editing**
   - Sentence structure variety
   - Word choice precision
   - Redundancy elimination
   - Active voice preference

3. **Copy Editing**
   - Grammar & punctuation
   - Spelling consistency
   - Style guide adherence
   - Final proof-reading

## Franchise DNA Analysis

### **Marvel Cinematic Universe**
**Structural Pattern**: Individual origin → team assembly → crossover events
**Character Development**: Flawed heroes dengan realistic relationships
**Theme Integration**: Family, sacrifice, redemption
**Learning Point**: Long-term character investment pays off

### **Game of Thrones (A Song of Ice and Fire)**
**Structural Pattern**: Multiple POV dengan political intrigue
**Character Development**: Morally gray characters dengan realistic consequences
**Theme Integration**: Power corrupts, consequences of actions
**Learning Point**: Complex world-building dengan political depth

### **Studio Ghibli Films**
**Structural Pattern**: Coming-of-age dengan magical elements
**Character Development**: Young protagonists dengan agency
**Theme Integration**: Environmental consciousness, human connection
**Learning Point**: Universal themes through fantastical lens
